Bank Customer Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

Overview:
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze over 10,000+ bank customer records.
The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, loan distribution, and marketing campaign performance to support data-driven decision-making.

Dataset:
1. The dataset contains customer-level banking information including:
2. Customer demographics (Education, Marital Status, Job)
3. Loan details (Personal Loan, Housing Loan)
4. Campaign details (Number of Calls, Duration, Outcome)
5. Subscription status
6. Total records analyzed: 10K+ customers

Tools & Technologies
1.Power BI
2.DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
3.Data Modeling
4.Data Visualization Techniques

Dashboard Features
🔹 KPI Cards
1.Total Customers
2.Total Balance
3.Loan Customers
4.Housing Loan Customers
5.Subscribed Customers
6.Total Campaign Calls

🔹 Visual Analysis
1.Customer Distribution by Education
2.Customer Distribution by Marital Status
3.Loan Distribution by Job Role
4.Campaign Duration by Outcome
5.Interactive Filters (Education, Marital Status)

Key Insights
1.Housing loans are the most popular financial product.
2.Loan penetration is relatively low compared to total customers.
3.Campaign calls are high, but subscription conversion rate is low.
4.Customers with secondary education form the largest segment.
5.Management and technician job roles show higher loan adoption.
